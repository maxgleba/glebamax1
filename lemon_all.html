Agridera Fresh Market lemon hybrids

×××××

Agridera aspires to be among the global leaders in the cultivation, production, and distribution of lemon seeds. Our dedication to research and development ensures solutions for growers and consumers worldwide. We specialize in creating disease-resistant varieties tailored to diverse markets.

Presently, our efforts concentrate on integrating resistances to diseases like Citrus Canker and Lemon Scab, alongside other citrus-borne pathogens, to develop superior lemon varieties. Additionally, we're actively working on enhancing resistance against emerging threats like Lemon Yellow Vein Clearing Virus.

Our breeding programs prioritize traits such as early maturity, varied fruit sizes, firmness, vibrant colors, flavor, resistance to drying, extended shelf life, and adaptability to various temperature ranges. We aim to offer a range of lemons, including traditional round varieties and elongated shapes, with a focus on quality and disease resilience.

Agridera's lemon breeding programs concentrate on three primary categories: Eureka Lemon, Meyer Lemon, and Lisbon Lemon.

Eureka Lemon Breeding Program

This program targets markets demanding high-quality lemons with thick rind, juicy pulp, and excellent flavor. Our focus is on developing varieties suitable for both fresh consumption and culinary uses.

Meyer Lemon Breeding Program

Geared towards regions with subtropical climates, such as California and parts of Florida. We prioritize disease-resistant varieties, emphasizing resistance to common citrus diseases, while ensuring superior taste and fragrance.

Lisbon Lemon Breeding Program

Tailored for markets valuing classic lemon flavors and versatility. We aim to create varieties with tangy, aromatic taste profiles and thick peel, suitable for juicing, cooking, or fresh consumption.

Agridera operates through international and local distributors around the world.
